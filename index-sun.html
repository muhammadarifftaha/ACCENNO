How to play

AI Turn ...

1. Select any available space to place your marker.
Available spaces will be highlighted as you hover over them.

2. Click once to highlight the space.
Clicking any other space will deselect your previous selection.
To deselect, click on the selected space.

3. Double-click to confirm your selection.
Alternatively, you can also double-click without first selecting if you're confident with your selection.

4. Wait for your turn again.
The AI or P2 will take their turn. At the ned of it, it will be your turn again.
